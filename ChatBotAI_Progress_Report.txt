PROJECT OVERVIEW
================================================================================
ChatBotAI is a unified messaging system for vacation rental hosts that
aggregates guest communications from multiple platforms (Email, WhatsApp,
Airbnb, Booking.com) and uses local AI (Ollama) to provide intelligent,
personalized auto-responses with persistent guest memory.

Location: C:\Users\admin\Documents\FlaskApp\ChatBotAI
Integration: COMPLETED - Added as 7th menu option in FlaskApp homepage
AI Server: Ollama at http://localhost:11434
AI Model: mistral:7b-instruct

BREAKTHROUGH FEATURE: Persistent Guest Memory
- The system remembers EVERYTHING about every guest permanently
- Extracts information from ALL messages (guest AND owner)
- Creates truly personalized service for returning guests
